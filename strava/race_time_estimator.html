Info
Assumptions: carbs at 75 g/hour, water at 750 ml/hour for a high sweat rate, sodium target at 500 mg/hour, carrying up to two 750 ml bottles before using the water pack.
The mix: 1 scoop Gatorade, 1 scoop sugar, 1 scoop maltodextrin, 1 SaltStick capsule, pinch of salt, blended. Default is 3 scoops of mix per 750 ml bottle.
Per bottle: about 53 g carbs and about 471 mg sodium.
Bottle sodium math: sodium scales with scoops per bottle. The full 3-scoop batch has about 106 mg from Gatorade + 215 mg from SaltStick + 150 mg from a light pinch of salt = 471 mg sodium.
Salt math: salt rows are shown as table-salt equivalent. Sodium is converted to salt with about 2.54 g salt per 1 g sodium.
Gatorade powder: 27 g carbs per 29 g powder, 170 mg sodium per 29 g powder. One 18 g scoop is about 16.8 g carbs and 106 mg sodium.
Sugar: 1 scoop = 18 g carbs. Maltodextrin: 1 scoop = 18 g carbs.
SaltStick: 1 capsule = 215 mg sodium. Add a capsule hourly on hot days or heavy sweat days.
Haribos: 17 pieces = 40 g, 31 g carbs. 1 piece = 1.8 g carbs. 1 bag = 150 g, 116 g carbs, about 64 gummies.
Water and salt depend on heat and sweat rate; carbs are the steady target.
